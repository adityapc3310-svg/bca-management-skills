Core Competencies

Five Pillars of BCA Management

Each skill combines theoretical frameworks with practical applications — preparing you for real-world tech leadership roles.

Business Communication

Master professional writing, presentations, cross-cultural communication, and stakeholder management. Learn to translate technical concepts for non-technical audiences.

Professional Writing Presentation Skills Stakeholder Management Technical Translation

Project Management

Agile, Scrum, Kanban, Waterfall methodologies. Sprint planning, risk management, resource allocation, and delivery excellence using industry-standard tools like Jira, Trello, and Asana.

Agile & Scrum Risk Management Sprint Planning Jira & Trello

Leadership & Team Dynamics

Develop emotional intelligence, conflict resolution, delegation, motivation techniques, and inclusive leadership. Build high-performing teams in diverse tech environments.

Emotional Intelligence Conflict Resolution Delegation Team Building

Strategic Planning

SWOT analysis, OKRs, KPIs, roadmapping, competitive analysis, and data-driven decision making. Align technology initiatives with business objectives.

SWOT Analysis OKRs & KPIs Roadmapping Competitive Analysis

Organizational Behavior

Understand motivation theories, organizational culture, change management, power dynamics, and decision-making psychology in technology organizations.

Motivation Theories Change Management Org Culture Decision Psychology

Integrated Capstone

Synthesize all five pillars in a real-world consulting project. Work with industry partners to solve actual business-technology challenges end-to-end.

Industry Projects Client Engagement Portfolio Building Career Prep
Course Structure

Semester-wise Curriculum

Progressive learning path from fundamentals to advanced applications — each semester builds on the previous.

Semester 1: Foundations

Business Communication Fundamentals • Management Principles • Organizational Behavior Basics • Professional Ethics

Semester 2: Core Skills

Project Management (Agile/Waterfall) • Leadership Essentials • Strategic Thinking • Business Analytics Basics

Semester 3: Advanced Applications

Advanced Project Management • Change Management • Negotiation & Conflict Resolution • Digital Transformation

Semester 4: Capstone & Career

Integrated Capstone Project • Industry Internship • Portfolio Development • Interview Preparation & Placement

Elective Specializations

Choose from: Tech Product Management • IT Consulting • Entrepreneurship • Data-Driven Decision Making • Cybersecurity Management

Certifications Included

Scrum Master (PSM I) • Project Management (CAPM) • Business Analysis (ECBA) • Leadership Assessment (DISC) • Six Sigma Yellow Belt
